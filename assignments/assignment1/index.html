Card Games


Yugioh

Yugioh is a card game originating from Japan. Two main formats, OCG for most of Asia and TCG for most of the west. Both formats have different banlists. It has a digital version called Master Duel with its own banlist.

Meta Decks

  • Snake-Eyes
  • Tenpai
  • Yubel

Magic The Gathering

Magic The Gathering, or MTG for shorthand, is a card game made in the United States. MTG cards generally can be organized into 5 colors, with multicolor being different combinations. MTG has a rotating format based on sets called Standard. One of the most popular alternate format is commander.
